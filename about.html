About the author

Lin-Manuel Miranda is an American composer, lyricist, and actor known for creating and starring in the musical Hamilton.

Inspired by Ron Chernow’s biography, he developed the show in 2009, blending hip-hop with traditional musical styles. His portrayal of Hamilton highlighted themes of ambition and resilience, earning him a Tony Award, Pulitzer Prize, and Grammy Award.

Though he stepped back from the role in 2016, Miranda continues to support *Hamilton* and its cultural impact.
