Characters

Alexander Hamilton

A driven immigrant who becomes a key figure in shaping America. His ambition and brilliance in finance drive him through political challenges and rivalries, particularly with Thomas Jefferson, revealing both his ideals and personal sacrifices.

Played by: Lin Manuel Miranda

Aaron Burr

Aaron Burr is an ambitious yet cautious politician who serves as a foil to Hamilton. His rivalry and desire for power lead to their fateful duel, revealing his internal conflict and regret over the choices he makes.

Played by: Leslie Odom, Jr.

Angelica Schuyler

Angelica Schuyler is a sharp and strong-willed woman who shares a deep connection with Alexander Hamilton. She prioritizes her sister Eliza's happiness over her own feelings, showcasing her selflessness and reflecting the challenges women faced in a patriarchal society.

Played by: Renée Elise Goldsberry.

Eliza Schuyler

Eliza Schuyler is Alexander Hamilton's devoted wife, embodying strength and resilience. She supports his ambitions and makes significant sacrifices, ultimately striving to preserve his legacy after his death.

Played by: Phillipa Anne Soo.

Thomas Jefferson

Thomas Jefferson is an articulate and ambitious politician who serves as a rival to Alexander Hamilton. He opposes Hamilton's financial policies, showcasing his commitment to democratic ideals and reflecting the ideological divides in early American politics.

Played by: Daveed Diggs.

George Washington

George Washington is depicted as a wise mentor to Alexander Hamilton and the first President of the United States. He embodies unity and strength, navigating the challenges of a new nation while highlighting the burdens of leadership and a pragmatic approach to governance.

Played by: Christopher Jackson.
